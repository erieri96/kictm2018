About

MediaCon is one of the main events of KICTM which hosts a contest for fledgling game developers from across Malaysia. Be it 3D or 2D, all genres and platforms are welcome. The most creative shall grab the main prize.

So grab your team of up to 4 people or going solo and join this annual contest now!
